Heritage Sites

Triveni Sangam

The holy confluence of the Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers, a sacred pilgrimage site.

Allahabad Fort

An architectural marvel built by Emperor Akbar, overlooking the Sangam.

Anand Bhavan

The ancestral home of the Nehru family, now a museum depicting India's freedom struggle.

Cultural Highlights

Kumbh Mela

Witness the world's largest religious gathering, held every 12 years at the Sangam.

Magh Mela

A smaller yet significant festival that attracts millions of pilgrims annually.

Natural Attractions

Khusro Bagh

A historic garden and burial complex with beautiful Mughal architecture.

Yamuna River

Enjoy a serene boat ride along the Yamuna, offering stunning views of the city.
